Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard

This project brings together sales, profitability, and customer insights into one interactive Power BI experience. It helps stakeholders quickly understand which products, regions, and customer segments are driving meaningful growth and margin.

Date Completed: March 2026 Portfolio Project
BIPower BI DBSQL XLExcel DXDAX

Project Overview

Business intelligence dashboard

The dashboard was built to turn a large sales dataset into a clear view of how revenue and profitability move across categories, regions, and customer groups. It combines performance tracking with visual storytelling so business users can quickly identify which areas deserve more focus.

Business Problem

Why the work mattered

The business needed a more reliable way to understand which sales efforts were actually contributing to profit. Static reports were too slow and lacked a direct view of how products, locations, and customer segments were performing in relation to margin.

💰

Data Preparation

Cleaning and shaping the data
Removed duplicate records
Checked missing values
Standardized data formats
Verified data types
Created calculated fields for profit and growth

Key Insights

What the analysis revealed

High-performing regions

A small set of regions accounted for a large share of revenue and profitability, showing where the strongest demand was concentrated.

Margin pressure in select segments

Several high-volume segments showed lower profit margins, indicating that growth alone was not a reliable signal of business value.

Recommendations

Next steps for decision makers

Prioritize high-margin regions

Increase focus on the regions producing the strongest profit contribution while monitoring lower-margin areas more closely.

Rebalance product mix

Shift promotional effort toward products that show stronger profitability and better long-term value.

Refine customer targeting

Use customer segment insights to align sales and support actions with the segments that create the highest value.

Track margin trends regularly

Use the dashboard as an ongoing review tool so profitability issues are visible before they become major risks.
